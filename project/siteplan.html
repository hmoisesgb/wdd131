Site Plan

Site Name

Climbing the Ranked Ladder

This website focuses on my personal ranked League of Legends experience and the lessons I have learned while climbing the ranked ladder.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about League of Legends, explain ranked gameplay, and share tips and strategies that helped me improve over the years.

Scenarios

  • What should I know before playing ranked games?
  • What common mistakes prevent players from climbing?
  • Which champions are best for beginners?
  • What strategies helped you improve your rank?
